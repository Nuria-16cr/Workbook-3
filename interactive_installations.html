Interactive installations

DUNE

DUNE is an interactive public artwork made of thousands of LED fibers that respond to the sound and movement of passing visitors. This installation is reminiscent of the film avatar when glowing lights open and close as they pass by. This behaviour is also seen in sea creatures like certain corals that close when the sense movement nearby. The installation was also presented outdoors, creating wonder environment it an otherwise urban space.

Pulse Room

The Pulse Room is an interactive installation made up of three hundred clear light bulbs, each and hung from a. There are interfaced located around the room that when a participant holds, the computer will detect their heartbeat and start flashing the nearest light bulb at the same rhythm of their heart.
