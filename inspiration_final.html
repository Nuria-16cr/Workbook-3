Inspiration for final project


Useful teddy bears

Baby's best companion while also checking their temperature, heart rate and other vital signs which are still in development. Based off the idea that smart watches can track so much now a days, Teddy the guardian is especially helpful at hospitals to soothe children while still collecting the necessary data or at home if the kid is ill and parents must keep a constant track of the kids temperature.

The Hug

Designed for people with dementia The Hug helps relieve anxious, lonely or distressed feeling. Derived from the need for human touch and connection in hospitals specially for long term patients and illnesses affecting the brain, The hug is a soothing toy that can play music, has a heartbeat and weighted arms to the experience feels more human and can provide further comfort.
